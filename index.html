Welcome to the Civic Issue Reporting & Resolution System

Welcome to Civic Issue Reporting System — a smarter way to build better cities. We believe every pothole fixed, every streetlight restored, and every clean street makes a difference. This platform empowers you to report everyday civic issues in real time, track their progress, and work together with local authorities to create cleaner, greener, and more responsive communities. Let’s make our cities thrive — one report at a time!
